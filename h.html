Description

This 2D map shows areas, highways, major roads, free zones, communities, islands, beaches, parks, shopping malls, golf courses, attractions, schools, hospitals, hotels and resorts, heritage sites etc., within the Emirate of Abu Dhabi. This horizontal map features the mainland and islands surrounding it all the way to Al Shamkha. Currently, the map is available in English but can be customized in other foreign languages as per request. It serves as an excellent wall map to dress your office or home walls.

Map spreads across:
From left to right: Massnoua Island, Al Ras Al Akhdar, Marina Village to Al Falah, Warsan Farms, Al Shamkha South
From top to bottom: Nurai Island, Ras Ghurab Island, Al Weheil Island to Mussafah, Mohammed bin Zayed City, Al Mafraq, Baniyas

Additional Information

Map Details

  • Our maps are printed on vinyl material which last long
  • Glossy laminated maps are good if you need to use whiteboard markers on them as you can erase data when not required
  • Matt lamination is most recommended if it is for wall mounting or framing purposes

Installation

  • Installation charges for wall or customized maps start from AED 250

Shipping and Delivery

  • Delivery in Dubai is complimentary
  • Delivery in the UAE will be at an additional charge of AED 25
  • International shipping charges will apply to only roll up maps (foam board excluded). Contact us for a quote
